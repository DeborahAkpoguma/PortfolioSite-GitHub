Introduction

The COVID-19 pandemic has had a significant impact on global health, economies, and societies. To gain insights into the spread of the virus and the effectiveness of vaccination efforts, analyzing COVID-19 data is crucial.

Target Audience

The target audience for this project includes policymakers, public health officials, researchers, or anyone interested in understanding the impact of COVID-19 and the progress of vaccination efforts in different countries.

Dataset

The data used in this project can be found here. The dataset includes data collected from January 1, 2020 to April 30, 2023.

The data was split into two datasets:

  • covid_deaths
  • covid_vaccinations
The datasets were then imported into SQL where the data was explored and analayzed.

Questions to be Answered
  1. What is the overall mortality rate based on the total cases and total deaths?
  2. Which countries have the highest number of COVID-19 cases and deaths?
  3. What is the average number of new tests conducted per day/week/month?
  4. How does the vaccination rate correlate with the number of COVID-19 cases and deaths?
  5. Which countries have the highest and lowest vaccination rates?
  6. What is the percentage of the population that has been fully vaccinated?
  7. How do ICU admissions and hospital patient numbers change over time?
